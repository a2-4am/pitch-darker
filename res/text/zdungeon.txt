 ZORK (DUNGEON) is a game of adventure, danger, and low cunning. In it you
 will explore some of the most amazing territory ever seen by mortal man.
 Hardened adventurers have run screaming from the terrors contained within!
 
 In ZORK the intrepid explorer delves into the forgotten secrets of a lost
 labyrinth deep in the bowels of the earth, searching for vast treasures
 long hidden from prying eyes, treasures guarded by fearsome monsters and
 diabolical traps!
